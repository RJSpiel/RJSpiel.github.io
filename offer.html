Common Questions

THINGS PEOPLE
USUALLY ASK

How is ARES different from searching CoStar myself?

CoStar lists commercial properties broadly — it doesn't filter for automotive zoning compliance, EPA soil history, or lot configurations that work for service bays. Using it for an automotive search means chasing compliance information on every listing yourself, often at the point where money is already committed. ARES does that screening first.

When does the engagement fee apply?

ARES offers platform access for independent searching and a full scouting engagement for hands-on support. Pricing is discussed before any commitment is made — there is no cost to make an initial enquiry or book a discovery call.

Do I need my own commercial broker?

ARES is not a brokerage and does not replace a commercial broker for the transaction itself. We handle the compliance screening, then connect you with brokers in our network who specialise in automotive real estate — so you get a specialist, not a generalist who is learning your industry alongside you.
